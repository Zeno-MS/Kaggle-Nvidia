# Experiment Tracker

> Every experiment gets logged here. No experiment is too small.
> The goal is to build a searchable history of what we tried, what happened,
> and what it means for the strategy.

---

## Experiment Template

```markdown
### EXP-[number]: [Short title]
**Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
**Phase**: 0/1/2/3
**Track**: A (Symbolic) / B (Judge) / C (Neural) / General
**Hypothesis**: What we expected to happen
**Setup**: What we did (brief but reproducible)
**Results**: What actually happened (include numbers)
**Interpretation**: What this means
**Strategy impact**: Does this change anything in STRATEGY.md?
**Assumptions affected**: [link to ASSUMPTIONS.md IDs]
**Follow-up**: What to try next based on this
**Compute used**: [Colab / Kaggle / local]
**Time spent**: [approximate]
```
